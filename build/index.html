Notes

Escape information-overload and wait for a non-event. Sure, it might not really happen—but hey—who cares...(?)

Simulations and virtual environments are directly informed by nature, and are thus an extension of it, rather than mere attempts at replication. ‘At Some Point This Tree Will Fall’ explores the use of these technologies as a way of reconnecting with nature, and seeks to validate it as such.

Jack Wild, 2018
